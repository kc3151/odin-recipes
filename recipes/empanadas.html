Beef Empanadas

Description

Argentinian-style beef empanadas from Bon Apppetit Test Kitchen Manager Gaby Melian.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp EVOO
  • 2 lb. ground beef
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • 2 red bell peppers

Steps

  1. Heat oil over high, cook beef until brown, 6-8 mins
  2. Reduce heat to medium, cook onions and bell peppers, add spices and stock
  3. Add filling to pastries, fold and crimp
  4. Fry on high heat until golden brown
